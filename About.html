About Me

As you already know, my name is Brandon Bass and I am a GIMM student at Boise State University. I grew up in the coastal city of Encinitas, California.

At a young age, I have always been intrigued by computers. I remember, even back then, how much I wanted to learn how to make things on the Virtual Web 2.0.

Fast forward a decade or so and now I have finally ended up here. Learning exactly what I wanted to do at a young age. This website is to display who am I as a person and some of my works from my time here at school.
